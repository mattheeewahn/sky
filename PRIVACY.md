# Privacy Policy — SkyTrace

**Last updated:** June 2026

## What data SkyTrace collects

SkyTrace collects **no personal data**. All information stays on your device.

### Location
- Used solely to calculate positions of celestial objects in your local sky.
- Never transmitted to external servers.
- Never stored beyond the current session (unless you save it in an observation record).

### Photos and files
- Accessed only when you explicitly choose to load images for blink comparison or attach photos to observations.
- Never uploaded anywhere.

### Network requests
SkyTrace makes network requests **only** to download public astronomy catalog data:
- **CelesTrak** (celestrak.org) — Satellite orbital elements
- **NASA JPL** (ssd-api.jpl.nasa.gov) — Asteroid/comet data
- **Minor Planet Center** (minorplanetcenter.net) — Asteroid orbits

No user data, location, or personal information is included in these requests.

### Observation records
- Stored in a local database on your device.
- You control all exports (CSV, JSON).
- Nothing is shared unless you explicitly choose to export.

## Third-party services
- SkyTrace does not use analytics services.
- SkyTrace does not use advertising SDKs.
- SkyTrace does not use crash reporting services that transmit personal data.

## Children's privacy
SkyTrace does not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

## Changes to this policy
Any changes will be posted in this document and reflected in app updates.
